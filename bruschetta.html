Bruschetta

Price: R150

Serving Size: 3 portion

Bruschetta is an antipasto from Italy consisting of grilled bread rubbed with garlic and topped with olive oil and salt. Variations may include toppings of tomato, vegetables, beans, cured meat, or cheese. In Italy, bruschetta is often prepared using a brustolina grill. The Bruschetta is one of our most famous starters and a favourite of our customers.

Extras:

  • Sweet potato fries
  • Herb-infused olive oil dip
  • Extra tomatoes
  • Feta cheese crumbles
  • Balsamic glaze drizzle
