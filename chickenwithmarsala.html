Chicken with Marsala Risotto

Price: R350

Serving Size: 1 portion

Chicken Marsala is a classic Italian-American dish that has become a staple in Italian restaurants around the world. It is usually made with a hint of sherry, but in this recipe, you already have the marsala taste in the chicken and mushrooms, which the risotto absorbs. This delightful combination of tender chicken, earthy mushrooms, and creamy marsala risotto will transport you to the heart of Italy.

Extras:

  • Truffle oil drizzle
  • Asparagus spears
  • Parmesan cheese shavings
  • Sage-infused butter sauce
  • Crushed pistachios
